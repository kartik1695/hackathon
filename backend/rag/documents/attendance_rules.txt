Attendance Rules

Statuses
- PRESENT
- WFH
- ABSENT

Logging
- Attendance is tracked per employee per date.
- A check-in may be updated if already present; do not create duplicates for the same date.

Anomalies
- LATE: repeated late arrivals
- MISSING: missing check-out or missing expected record
- OTHER: other operational issues

Burnout Guidance
- Anomalies and repeated absences can be early indicators of burnout risk.
- Escalate to manager/HR for support-oriented follow-up.
