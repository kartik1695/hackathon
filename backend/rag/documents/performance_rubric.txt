Performance Review Rubric

Goals
- Each goal has a title, description, status, and timestamps.
- Status examples: OPEN, IN_PROGRESS, DONE, BLOCKED.

Review Cycles
- Review cycles cover a specific period (period_start to period_end).
- Drafts should summarize goals, key outcomes, and risks in a neutral tone.

Writing Guidance
- Use objective facts from goals and attendance signals.
- Avoid sensitive personal data.
- Provide actionable feedback and next steps where possible.
